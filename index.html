Why Be Digital?

A simple service focused on clarity, trust and conversion — built as part of a Business Management project.

Designed with psychology in mind

Colours, spacing and call-to-action buttons are chosen to be calm but clear. The dark green background supports trust, while lime accents highlight prices and actions without looking too aggressive.

Sections follow a logical journey: first understand the offer, then see the services, then learn why to trust the brand, and finally how the process works.

Focused on real small-business problems

Be Digital is aimed at people who know they need a website but feel overwhelmed by tools and costs. Packages are straightforward, and communication is kept friendly and simple.
